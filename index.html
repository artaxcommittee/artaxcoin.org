PoS and Masternodes

The ARTAX network is secured through Staking and Backend Encryption Cache Persistence(ECP). Artax uses incentivized Masternodes for services such as Instant transactions and Governance. Each node requires 2500 XAX as collateral. They receive 80% of the PoS block rewards. 20% going to stakers. Random reward integer for Masternodes much like finding a block.

Private

ARTAX has the allowances for both I2P and Tor Integration. The ARTAX Encryption Cache Technology that is being developed.

Security

ARTAX uses high volume scrypt hashing to protect blockchain network. The idea of ARTAX is to re-purpose high-volume hashes in both ARTAX to integrate encryption cache persistence and node streamlining through one spectrum.
